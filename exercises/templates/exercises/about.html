About Workouts App

Welcome to the Workouts App, your go-to platform for discovering and sharing exercise routines!

Our mission is to provide a reliable and collaborative fitness resource, empowering users to create, explore, and benefit from a wide range of exercises tailored to their fitness goals.

Each exercise entry is carefully moderated by our dedicated team to ensure content quality and accuracy. Only approved exercises make it to the home page, offering users a curated selection of verified workout routines.

Whether you're a fitness enthusiast or a beginner, the Workouts App welcomes you to join our community and contribute to a healthier and active lifestyle.
